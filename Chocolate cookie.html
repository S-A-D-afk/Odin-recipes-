How to make chocolate chip cookies

Description

In this tutorial I'm going to teach you how to make a homemade chocolate chip cookie in steps but firstly the ingredients we need is listed below

Ingredients(Per 20 cookies)

  • 1 cup (225g) unsalted butter,softened
  • 1 cup (200g) white sugar
  • 1 cup (200g) brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3 cups (360g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups (340g) chocolate chips

Procedure in steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper
  2. Cream butter & sugars: In a large bowl, beat the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth and fluffy
  3. Add eggs & vanilla: Mix in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract
  4. Mix dry ingredients: In another bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt
  5. Combine wet & dry: Slowly add the dry mixture into the butter-sugar mixture. Stir until just combined
  6. Add chocolate chips: Fold in the chocolate chips gently
  7. Scoop dough: Drop spoonfuls of dough (about 1–2 tbsp each) onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving space between them
  8. Bake for 8–12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown but the centers are still soft
  9. Cool & enjoy: Let cookies rest on the baking sheet for 2 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack
Serve and enjoy
And viola,your chocolate chip cookie is done and ready to be munched(pro tip:Goes better with orange juice)
